About

Strings Attached, Texas is a music entertainment company comprised of the most talented classical musicians. With an average performance schedule of 200+ engagements per year, Strings Attached has serenaded guests for concerts, corporate events, galas, holiday parties, proposals and weddings throughout Texas and Louisiana. And we would love to help you create a customized music program that will perfectly fit your style, taste, and budget!

As a music agency, we offer live performances in a wide variety of ensembles types and range of styles. Solo, duet, trio, and quartet ensembles of traditional strings, harp, and/or piano that can play classical, cinema, jazz, pop and rock - our goal is to create a memorable musical experience for your event.

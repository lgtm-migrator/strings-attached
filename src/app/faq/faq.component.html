FAQ

Please email us for answers to further questions.

When should I contact you to book an ensemble for my event?

Although we can occasionally accommodate last-minute bookings, we recommend that you contact us 6 months in advance.

What do I need to provide at the event venue?

Depending on the ensemble, we will need armless chairs for the musicians and a space to play. Ideally for a string quartet, the performance space should be at least 10’x10’ with nice lighting. The musicians will bring their own instruments, music stands, and music.

Can you play outside?

Yes, but with restrictions. In general, we cannot perform in any sort of rain and the temperature must be between 70 and 90 degrees. During the summer, the musicians must have covering from the sun. The reasoning for these policies is to protect the instruments and musicians. If your event is outside, please arrange an indoor contingency plan.

What will the musicians wear?

We can dress to fit the occasion. Usually we wear all black, but we can dress in formal tuxedoes for men and dresses for women, or less formal.
